# Hypothesis Testing in Drug Safety Analysis

## Overview

In this project, I analyzed data from a randomized controlled drug trial to investigate the safety of a pharmaceutical treatment. Using Python, I compared the treatment and placebo groups to determine whether adverse effects, the number of adverse effects, and participant ages differed significantly. Appropriate statistical tests were selected based on the characteristics of the data.

## Key Insights

* Compared the proportion of participants experiencing adverse effects between the drug and placebo groups using a two-sample proportion test.
* Evaluated whether the number of adverse effects was associated with treatment group using a Chi-square test of independence.
* Assessed the distribution of participant ages to determine whether parametric testing assumptions were satisfied.
* Applied the Mann–Whitney U test to compare participant ages between the treatment and placebo groups.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Data preprocessing with Pandas
* Statistical hypothesis testing
* Chi-square test of independence
* Mann–Whitney U test
* Two-sample proportion test
