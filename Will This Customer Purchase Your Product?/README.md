# Analyzing Online Shopping Behavior During the Holiday Season

## Overview

In this project, I analyzed online shopping session data from November and December to better understand customer purchasing behavior during the busiest shopping months. Using Python, I compared purchase rates between new and returning customers, explored relationships between browsing activities, and applied probability modeling to estimate the likelihood of reaching a sales target after increasing the purchase rate of returning customers.

## Key Insights

* Calculated and compared purchase rates for new and returning customers.
* Measured correlations between administrative, informational, and product-related browsing durations.
* Identified the strongest relationship among browsing activity variables.
* Modeled a 15% increase in the purchase rate of returning customers.
* Applied the binomial distribution to estimate the probability of achieving at least **100 sales** out of **500 customer sessions**.
* Visualized the probability distribution of expected sales outcomes.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Data analysis with Pandas
* Correlation analysis
* Probability modeling with SciPy
* Data visualization with Matplotlib
